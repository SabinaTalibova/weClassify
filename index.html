About "weClassify"

Objective of Project

We Classify is a our Senior Design Project. Project is led by the supervision of Prof. Abzetdin Adamov. Main objective of our project is to create web based News Classifier. Classifier will be able to classify given news content in 15 categories.

Significance of Project

There are plenty of research on classification of texts in english.However, it is hard to find qualfied and trained datasets for azerbaijani language. Present classifiers on web that we searched are mostly in english and in other well known languages like German, Arabic, Spanish,Japan and so on. But there is no any classifier in Azerbaijani. Development of “We Classify” will not only help to classifiy web content it will also provide open and labaled data source for other researches. By Some changes and modifications to application, it will be easy for pther researchers to adjust classifier to other classification problems.

Future Plans

As a "We Classify" team we are planning to broaden the boundaries of our project to different classification problems such as Sentiment Classification
